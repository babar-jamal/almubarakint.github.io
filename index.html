About Us


Al Mubarak International SMC-Pvt Limited is newly registered LPG(Liquefied Petroleum Gas) Company with aim to ensure that LPG is made available to every corner of the country through a highly efficient distribution network - starting with district Sargodha.


At Al Mubarak International, we recognize the growing demand for LPG as a preferred domestic and commercial fuel in areas without access to natural gas distribution. This need is not limited to remote regions, but is also evident in major cities such as Bhalwal(Sargodha), where the shortage of LNG/CNG supplies has increased the demand for LPG. With this in mind, we are focused on establishing LPG storage and filling facilities to ensure a steady supply of cooking fuel to these areas.
The recent surge in LPG imports, with a 40% increase observed in just one year, highlights the tremendous potential of the LPG market in the energy sector. We believe that with the right support and policies from the government, LPG has the potential to become a major player in the energy industry in the years to come.
